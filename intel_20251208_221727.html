Young Genius Defies Boundaries: Singh's Drone Record Shatters Expectations

REGION: india | 2025-12-08 22:17
In a groundbreaking feat that has sent shockwaves through the tech world, Mehar Singh, a prodigious teen, has shattered the Guinness World Record for the fastest drone ascent. The audacious achievement was accomplished at the tender age of 16, sparking a global debate on youth innovation and its boundless potential.

The young maverick utilized his exceptional knowledge of aerodynamics to engineer a drone capable of surpassing previous records. In just 9.03 seconds, the drone soared from ground to sky, leaving the Guinness Book of World Records in its wake.

This technological marvel has not only elevated Singh to new heights of fame but has also ignited a worldwide interest in drone technology, paving the way for its commercial application. Singh's invention embodies the spirit of innovation, demonstrating that age is no barrier to shattering world records and rewriting the rules.
